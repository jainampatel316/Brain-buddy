1. What to Expect From Us

Your privacy is important to us. We use your data solely for providing and improving our services. Our principles:

- We only ask for necessary personal information.
- We don’t share your data unless required by law or for business development.
- We don’t store unnecessary personal information.
- We respond to requests promptly.

We may contact you occasionally for updates or feedback, ensuring communications are relevant.

2. Your Rights to Your Data

If you have an account, you can request exported data or request edits/deletion. This will include information you have provided through mechanisms such as contact forms, pop-ups, or special offer pages. You can also request that we edit or erase this information at any time. However, we may retain data for legal or security purposes.

3. Types of Data We Collect

We collect de-identified information and may release aggregated reports. We also collect information like IP addresses for logged-in users.

Common types include:

- Domain
- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Date and time of visit
- URLs of visited pages
- Usernames
- Information from voluntary entries.

We also collect information that may identify you, such as Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for logged in users and for users leaving comments on our blogs.

The amount and type of information we gather depends on the interaction. We may require:

Name
Email address
Phone number
Physical address
Cookies and usage data
Other details such as comments made on the contact form.
These details will be stored in a secure database and we may use them for future marketing efforts.

Our business does not address anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information form any children. If you have any concerns about this, please contact us.

4. How We Use Your Data

We use data for business operations, service improvement, customer support, and may use third-party analytics like Google Analytics and Facebook Pixels, ensuring the security of collected data.

5. How We Protect Your Data

We commit to protecting your data, disclosing it only to necessary parties. Retained data is deleted when no longer needed.

6. Privacy Policy Changes

We may update our policy, and changes will be posted here.
